The Wizard's Toolkit has been available to both free and commercial users since the early 2000's. In early 2003 it was one of the most comprehensive free cipher packages available, but, unlike competing packages, it continues to be supported and enhanced. Today, The Wizard's Toolkit is more comprehensive and better supported than ever.

The Wizard's Toolkit has enjoyed considerable support from commercial users. Since The Wizard's Toolkit is not restricted from use in commercial or proprietary applications, it is often used as a component of a number of commercial applications. Many of these commercial users have made contributions to The Wizard's Toolkit, large and small, often anonymous, but always appreciated.

The Wizard's Toolkit Studio LLC is now looking for sponsorship from individuals and companies which use The Wizard's Toolkit, or which would like to help support free software. If you or your company depends on The Wizard's Toolkit, sponsoring The Wizard's Toolkit is a way to express your gratitude and to contribute back to the further development of The Wizard's Toolkit.

The Wizard's Toolkit sponsorship can take many forms. The following are some possibilities:

  • Contribute source code or patches to help improve The Wizard's Toolkit.
  • Lend us developers to add support for additional encryption algorithms.
  • Provide technical writers to assist with the development of commercial-grade technical documentation.
  • Donate a workstation with a modern processor and plenty of memory and disk. We would use it for our primary development environment.
